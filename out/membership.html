Become a Member

Join a community dedicated to unity, progress, and Okpella development

Who Can Join

Persons of Okpella origin or descent

Spouses of Okpella indigenes

Individuals with strong interest in and commitment to Okpella culture and community development goals

Member Benefits

  • Voting rights in General Assembly (when in good standing)
  • Participate in organizational decision-making
  • Access to member-only events and meetings
  • Opportunity to serve on committees and leadership positions
  • Network with Okpella community members across USA and Canada
  • Direct involvement in community development projects
  • Support welfare assistance programs
  • Contribute to cultural preservation initiatives

Good Standing

Members in good standing enjoy full voting rights and participation privileges. Requirements include:

  • Current on annual membership dues
  • Regular meeting attendance or participation
  • Adherence to organizational bylaws and code of conduct
  • Active engagement in community initiatives

Membership Application

Application fee of $50 will be collected separately upon approval.
