How Decisions Are Made

Our governance structure ensures transparency, accountability, and member participation

General Assembly

The highest decision-making body, comprising all members in good standing.

Meets: Quarterly
Quorum: Simple majority of members in good standing

Executive Committee

Manages day-to-day operations and implements General Assembly decisions.

Meets: Monthly
