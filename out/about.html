About ODA–USA

ODA–USA focuses on projects beneficial to the entire Okpella community by collaborating with public/private partners to provide social and economic amenities and support investments that spur economic development and transform Okpella into a modern, vibrant town.

Our Origin & Purpose

Founded in 1999, the Okpella Development Association – USA brings together indigenes of Okpella residing in the United States and Canada. Our organization serves as a bridge between our community members across North America and our homeland, fostering unity and driving meaningful development.

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, we maintain the highest standards of transparency and accountability in all our operations. Our headquarters is located in Minneapolis, Minnesota, with members across the United States and Canada.

What We Do

Promote unity, peace, and harmony among Okpella indigenes in the USA and Canada

Support socio-economic development of Okpella and host communities

Advance education, youth development, and scholarship programs

Preserve and promote Okpella cultural heritage, traditions, and values

Engage investors with focus on fairness, environmental sustainability, and social/economic impact

Foster welfare assistance and community support programs

Maintain transparency and accountability in all operations

Commitment to Compliance & Transparency

ODA–USA maintains full compliance with IRS regulations for 501(c)(3) organizations. We are committed to transparent operations, regular financial reporting, and accountable stewardship of all donations and membership dues. All contributions are tax-deductible as allowed by law.
